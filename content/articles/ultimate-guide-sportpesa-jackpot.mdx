## What Is the SportPesa Jackpot?

The SportPesa Jackpot is a football prediction game where you predict the outcomes of 13 (Midweek) or 17 (Mega) football matches. Each match has three possible outcomes: Home Win, Draw, or Away Win. Get all predictions correct and you win the jackpot. Get close and you win a bonus.

The minimum stake is KSH 99. The jackpot prize pool is shared if multiple people win.

## Understanding the Odds

The mathematical probability of winning the Mega Jackpot is 1 in 129 million. The Midweek Jackpot is 1 in 1.6 million.
