## Case Study 1: The Nairobi Office Syndicate

**Structure:**
- 12 office colleagues
- KSh 500 each per month
- Total: KSh 6,000/month
- Entries: 15 slips per week

**Results (2024):**
- Bonus tiers: 8 hits (14-15/17 correct)
- Total winnings: KSh 240,000
- Each member profit: KSh 20,000 (200% ROI)

**Key success factor:** Consistent research, designated analyzer

## Case Study 2: University Friends Syndicate

**Structure:**
- 8 university friends
- KSh 200 each per week
- Focused only on Midweek (better odds)

**Results (2025):**
- Hit 12/13 three times
- Total winnings: KSh 180,000
- Each member profit: KSh 22,500
