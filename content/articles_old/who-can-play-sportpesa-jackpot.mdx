## Basic Requirements

✅ **Age:** 18 years or older
✅ **Residency:** Kenyan resident (citizen or legal resident)
✅ **ID:** Valid Kenyan ID or Passport
✅ **Phone:** Kenyan mobile number (Safaricom, Airtel, Telkom)
✅ **Payment:** M-Pesa account

## Age Verification

SportPesa requires:
- ID number during registration
- Photo of ID for KYC verification
- Date of birth verification

**Under 18?** You cannot play. Anyone caught using fake IDs risks permanent ban.

## Residency Rules

**Who CAN play:**
✅ Kenyan citizens
✅ Legal residents with valid permits
✅ Anyone with Kenyan ID

**Who CANNOT play:**
❌ Tourists (short-term visitors)
❌ Non-residents without Kenyan ID
❌ People in countries where SportPesa isn't licensed

## Multiple Accounts

**Not allowed.** One person = one account. Multiple accounts will be:
- Detected via ID verification
- Closed permanently
- Winnings forfeited

## Self-Exclusion

If you have gambling problems:
- SportPesa offers self-exclusion
- Contact support to voluntarily ban yourself
- Period: 6 months to permanent
