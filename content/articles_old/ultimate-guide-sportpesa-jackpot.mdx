## What Is the SportPesa Jackpot?

The SportPesa Jackpot is a football prediction game where you predict the outcomes of 13 (Midweek) or 17 (Mega) football matches. Each match has three possible outcomes: Home Win, Draw, or Away Win. Get all predictions correct and you win the jackpot. Get close and you win a bonus.

The minimum stake is **KSh 99**. The jackpot prize pool is shared if multiple people win. As of February 2026, the SportPesa Mega Jackpot has produced only **5 winners in history**, with the largest payout being **KSh 424.6 million** won by Bonnie Kamau in August 2025.

The mathematical probability of winning the Mega Jackpot is **1 in 129,140,163**. The Midweek Jackpot is **1 in 1,594,323** — still extremely unlikely, but 81 times better than the Mega.

## What to Do If You Win

Winning is just the beginning. Tax, investment, and financial planning are critical. Under Kenya's Finance Act 2025, you pay a **5% withholding tax** on all withdrawals from your betting wallet (down from the previous 20% on net winnings).

For a KSh 424 million jackpot win, the tax would be approximately **KSh 21.2 million** rather than the KSh 85 million it would have been under the old regime.
