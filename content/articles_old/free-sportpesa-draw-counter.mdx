## The Draw Distribution Trap

Many bettors make one of two mistakes:

### Mistake 1: Zero Draws (The "All Favorites" Trap)

Picking all favorites feels safe. But even if every game has a 60% favorite, the probability that ALL favorites win is only **0.60^17 ≈ 0.05%**. That's 1 in 2,000.

**The reality:** Upsets happen. In any given 17-game jackpot week, expect 2-4 upsets (draws or away wins against the favorite).

### Mistake 2: Too Many Draws (The "Play It Safe" Trap)

Some bettors think picking lots of draws reduces risk. But draws are actually rare outcomes:
- Premier League: 23% draw rate
- La Liga: 25% draw rate  
- Bundesliga: 23% draw rate

Having 8-10 draws in your slip means expecting draws to happen at **47-59%** frequency — nearly double the actual rate.

## The Mathematical Reality

Let's illustrate why draw distribution matters with some probability:

**Scenario A: Zero draws in a 17-game slip**
This assumes all 17 games will be decisive (home or away win). Historical data shows this happens in roughly **5-8%** of jackpot weeks. You're betting against a 92-95% likelihood.

**Scenario B: 3 draws in a 17-game slip**
This assumes 3 games (18%) will draw and 14 will be decisive. This aligns much closer to historical patterns (23-28% draws across leagues). You're working with reality, not against it.

**Scenario C: 9 draws in a 17-game slip**
This assumes 9 games (53%) will draw. This has **never happened in SportPesa jackpot history**. The highest confirmed draw count in a winning slip was 6.
