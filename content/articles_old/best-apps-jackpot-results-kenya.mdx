## SMS & USSD Checking

### SportPesa
**SMS:** Send anything to **79079** for jackpot results  
**USSD:** Dial ***790#** for results and betting  
**Cost:** Free (standard SMS/USSD rates)

### Betika
**SMS:** Send "RESULTS" to **29090**  
**USSD:** Dial ***644#**  
**Cost:** Free

### Odibets
**SMS:** Send "RESULTS" to **29680**  
**Cost:** Free

## "Bila Bundles" Zero-Data Betting

**Safaricom customers** can bet and check results **without data or airtime** using:

**SportPesa:** Available via *790# and app  
**Betika:** Available via *644# and app  
**How it works:** Transactions charged to M-Pesa, no bundles needed

**Perfect for:** Rural areas, users without smartphones

## Which Method Should You Use?

**Use official platform apps if:**
- You only play one platform
- You want betting + results in one app
- You need zero-data options

**Use third-party apps if:**
- You play multiple platforms
- You want fastest possible alerts
- You need detailed match stats for research

**Use SMS/USSD if:**
- You don't have a smartphone
- You're in area with poor data connection
- You want simple, reliable checking
